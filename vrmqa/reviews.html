Meta Quest

The Meta Quest is one of the best standalone Virtual reality headsets and can work as a PC VR. It is definately worth having and has a massive catalogue of fun and interesting games with an evergrowing community of people. It even has an app that allows you to purchase and manage all your games. The Meta Quest is great VR headset for people just starting their Virtual Reality journey or people who have already dabbled.

Bonelab

Bonelab is a fun action packed game with occasionaly annoying puzzles that make you feel rewarded after you complete them. The guns are realistic with a wide selection of random items you can hit the enemies with. It can be easily modded and has a very good physics system. there isn't much replayablity when it comes to the story however, the sanbox mode has ours of fun and often silly gameplay. I definitly suggest adding it your your library.
