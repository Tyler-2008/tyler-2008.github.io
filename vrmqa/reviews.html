Meta Quest

The Meta Quest is one of the best standalone Virtual reality headsets and can work as a PC VR. It is definately worth having and has a massive catalogue of fun and interesting games with an evergrowing community of people. It even has an app that allows you to purchase and manage all your games. The Meta Quest is great VR headset for people just starting their Virtual Reality journey or people who have already dabbled.

Bonelab

Bonelab is a fun action packed game with occasionaly (but very rarely) annoying puzzles that make you feel rewarded after you complete them. The guns are realistic with a wide selection of random items that you can hit the enemies with. It can be easily modded and has a very good physics system. there isn't much replayablity when it comes to the story however, the sanbox mode has hours of fun and often silly gameplay. I definitly suggest adding it your your library.

Superhot VR

Superhot VR is a fun game with almos matrix-like gameplay. It is really fun and you can try the demo here. The way it works is the slower you move the slower time moves and you have to fight your way to the end. After completing your first run-through you can replay as much as you want and even play other modes including speedrun, real time, no death and infinite waves. Again this one is definitly one worth having.
